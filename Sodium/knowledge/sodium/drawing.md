# Drawing — Sodium (Conceptual)

These drawings are **conceptual**: atoms are not tiny solar systems, but the simplified models are useful for communicating structure.

## 1) Bohr-style sodium atom (\(^{23}\text{Na}\), neutral)
- Nucleus: 11 protons, 12 neutrons
- Electrons by shell: **2–8–1**

```
                 e−
            .-----------.
         .-'             '-.
       .'   L shell (8e−)   '.
      /   e−   e−   e−   e−   \
```

## 2) A “piece of sodium metal” (how I imagine it)
Fresh sodium metal is often described as **silvery** and **soft**. Here’s a stylized sketch (not to scale):
