THERMAL DRIFT / 0-ICE PROTOCOL

Eat ice. Outrun intent.

Kamikaze ships are vectoring in. They don’t stop. You don’t win by destroying them — you win by staying alive.

Ice shards improve your handling on pickup.

Collect 3 shards to freeze enemies for a while and upgrade your ship.

After upgrade, you auto-shoot forward and each hit chips enemy HP.

Collect 3 more shards to unlock a cone shot.
